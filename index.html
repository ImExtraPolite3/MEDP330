DESTINY 2

What is Destiny 2 about?

In Destiny 2 players wield powerful abilities granted by a mysterious force called the "Light," bestowed by a large, mysterious entity called the Traveler. The Guardians' mission is to protect the last safe city on Earth and defend against hostile alien races such as the Fallen, Hive, Vex, and Cabal, as well as an evil force called the Darkness.
